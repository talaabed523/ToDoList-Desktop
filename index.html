        How to Use

        ✕

        Adding Tasks

        Type your task in the input box and click Add or press Enter. Optionally pick a due date before adding.

        Starring Tasks

        Click the ☆ star icon on any task to mark it as important. Starred tasks get a yellow border so they stand out.

        Subtasks

        Click the + icon on any task to open the subtasks panel. Add, complete, or delete subtasks for that task.

        Editing Tasks

        Click Edit on any task to change its name or due date inline. Press Enter or click away to save.

        Deleting Tasks

        Click Delete on any task to remove it. You will be asked to confirm before it's deleted.

        Deadlines

        Click Deadlines at the bottom to see all tasks with due dates and countdowns. Colors indicate urgency: 🟢 plenty of time, 🟡 less than 3 days, 🔴 overdue.

        Reset

        Click Reset to clear all tasks. This cannot be undone.

        Ask AI

        Click Ask AI in the bottom right to open the chatbot. It knows your tasks, subtasks, deadlines and can help you prioritize or plan.
